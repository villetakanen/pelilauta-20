## Surface Anchors

The surface palette spans from a near-black deep blue (`--chroma-surface-0`) to a near-white cool blue tint (`--chroma-surface-100`). All intermediate steps derive via `color-mix()`.

## Usage

- **Primary** — brand identity, interactive elements (buttons, links, FABs). The teal→yellow hue shift is unique to Cyan.
- **Secondary** — supporting accents, less prominent actions, tags and chips.
- **Surface** — backgrounds, cards, sheets, overlays. Use lower steps (0–30) for dark themes, higher steps (70–100) for light themes.

All derived steps use `color-mix()` — see the [chroma token spec](/specs/cyan-ds/tokens/chroma/spec.md) for implementation details.
