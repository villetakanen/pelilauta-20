Content Grids are the structural backbone of Cyan. They prioritize readability and deterministic spacing by utilizing a 3-column "Holy Grail" grid applied globally to `section` and `article` tags within the main content area.

## 1. Structural Logic

All content regions respond to the width of the parent `<main>` container using **Container Queries**.

- **Mobile:** Gutter padding of `--cn-gap` (16px) is enforced.
- **Desktop (>= 60rem):** Content is centered with a max-width of **`67ch`** (the optimal character measure for focused reading).

### Default Centering

By default, all children of a `section` or `article` are anchored to the central column without requiring manual utility classes.

<div class="cn-grid-debug" style="background: var(--cn-surface-1); border: 1px dashed var(--cn-border); padding: 1rem; border-radius: var(--cn-border-radius-medium);">
  <section class="cn-content-grid" style="background: var(--cn-surface-2); position: relative; border-radius: var(--cn-border-radius-small);">
    <small style="position: absolute; top: 4px; right: 8px; opacity: 0.5; font-size: 10px;">Grid Column 2</small>
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0;">Standard Section</h3>
    <p>This section is automatically centered to exactly 67ch on wide screens. No CSS classes were applied to this paragraph or header to achieve this alignment.</p>
  </section>
</div>

## 2. The Full-Width Bleed

When an element needs to break the 67ch constraint (e.g. for banners, large tables, or full-width imagery), use the **`.cn-grid-full`** class.

<div class="cn-grid-debug" style="background: var(--cn-surface-1); border: 1px dashed var(--cn-border); padding: 1rem; margin-top: 2rem; border-radius: var(--cn-border-radius-medium);">
  <section class="cn-content-grid" style="background: var(--cn-surface-2); border-radius: var(--cn-border-radius-small);">
    <p>I am centered (67ch).</p>
    <div class="cn-grid-full" style="background: var(--cn-button-accent); padding: 1rem; color: white; text-align: center;">
      <b>I am .cn-grid-full</b>
      <br/>(I span the entire width including the margins)
    </div>
    <p>I am centered again.</p>
  </section>
</div>

---

## 3. Vertical Rhythm

Direct siblings are automatically separated by **`2 * --cn-gap`** (32px) to maintain constant typographical density throughout long-form content.
