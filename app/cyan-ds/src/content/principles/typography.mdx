Cyan v20 delivers a high-impact, editorial typography system anchored to the **Lato** family. 

## 1. High-Contrast Base (17px)

To optimize Lato's legibility at modern screen resolutions, we use a **17px** base font size. While the browser root remains at 16px for compatibility, Cyan's primary text is defined as **1.0625rem**. This ensures that standard reading text remains clear and optically balanced.

---

## 2. Augmented Fourth (1.414) Scale

The hierarchy follows the intense rhythm of the **Augmented Fourth** modular scale. This creates a significant "punch" for headers while maintaining clearly distinguishable steps.

| Level | Size (px) | Line Height | Letter Spacing | Margin Bottom | Utility |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| <div class="text-h1">H1</div> | **4.25rem** (68px) | **1.294** (88px) | **-0.02em** | **1.5rem** (24px) | `.text-h1` |
| <div class="text-h2">H2</div> | **3.0rem** (48px) | **1.333** (64px) | **-0.01em** | **1.5rem** (24px) | `.text-h2` |
| <div class="text-h3">H3</div> | **2.125rem** (34px) | **1.412** (48px) | **0.01em** | **1.5rem** (24px) | `.text-h3` |
| <div class="text-h4">H4</div> | **1.5rem** (24px) | **1.333** (32px) | **normal** | **1.5rem** (24px) | `.text-h4` |
| <div class="text-bold">Body</div> | **1.0625rem** (17px) | **1.412** (24px) | **0.03em** | **1.0rem** (16px) | `.text-body` |

---

## 3. Responsive Fallbacks (Container Queries)

Each heading responds to its container width by shifting precisely one step down the scale. The H4 (24px) falls back to our **Base Bold (17px)** weight to preserve readability. This scaling is driven by **Container Queries** on the `main` or `article` element.

---

## 4. Vertical Rhythm (8px Grid)

Typography adheres to a deterministic **8px baseline grid**. 

- **Body Line-Height:** Fixed at **24px** (3 grid units) for optimal readability. 
- **Heading Line-Heights:** Anchored to specific grid multiples (88px, 64px, 48px, 32px) to ensure consistent flow, specifically tuned to contrast with the standard body line.
- **Letter Spacing:** Pushed significantly on large displays (H1-H2) to create an editorial, "punchy" aesthetic.

## 4. Article Relief

Cyan automatically applies "Relief" (margin-top: 0) to the leading heading inside any `article` element. This ensures that content remains perfectly aligned with UI containers.

<article style="background: var(--cn-surface-2); padding: 1.5rem; border: 1px solid var(--cn-border-color);">
  <h2 style="background: var(--cn-color-secondary-low); color: white; padding: 0.5rem;">Leading H2 (Relief HUD Active)</h2>
  <p>The standard top-margin of this H2 has been zeroed out to align with the parent container.</p>
</article>
