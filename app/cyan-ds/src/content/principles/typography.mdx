Cyan v20 delivers a high-impact, editorial typography system anchored to the **Lato** family. Our system prioritizing a high-contrast, "punchy" look by utilizing the **Augmented Fourth (1.414)** modular scale.

## 1. High-Contrast Base (17px)

To optimize Lato's legibility at modern screen resolutions, we use a **17px** base font size. While the browser root remains at 16px for compatibility, Cyan's primary text is defined as **1.0625rem**. This ensures that standard reading text remains clear and optically balanced.

---

## 2. Augmented Fourth (1.414) Scale

The hierarchy follows the intense rhythm of the Augmented Fourth scale. This creates a significant "punch" for headers while maintaining clearly distinguishable steps.

| Level | Scale Step | Desktop Size | Line Height | Mobile Fallback (< 620px) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **H1** | Step 4 | **4.25rem** (68px) | **1.25** | **3.0rem** (H2 style) |
| **H2** | Step 3 | **3.0rem** (48px) | **1.25** | **2.125rem** (H3 style) |
| **H3** | Step 2 | **2.125rem** (34px) | **1.5** | **1.5rem** (H4 style) |
| **H4** | Step 1 | **1.5rem** (24px) | **1.5** | **Base-Bold** (17px) |
| **Body** | Base | **1.0625rem** (17px)| **1.5** | **1.0625rem** |

---

## 3. Responsive Fallbacks (Container Queries)

Each heading responds to its container width by shifting precisely one step down the scale. H4 (24px) falls back to our **Base Bold (17px)** weight to preserve readability. This scaling is driven by **Container Queries** on the `main` or `article` element at a **620px** threshold.

---

## 4. Article Relief

Cyan automatically applies "Relief" (margin-top: 0) to it leading heading inside any `article` element. This ensures that content remains perfectly aligned with UI containers.

---

## 5. Contrast Matrix (WCAG AA)

Accessibility is non-negotiable. Our system enforces specific text colors based on surface elevation.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Elevation 4 Guardrail:** In Dark Mode, when a surface is **Elevation 4**, typography MUST use **Step 100 (White)** to maintain WCAG AA (4.5:1) compliance.
