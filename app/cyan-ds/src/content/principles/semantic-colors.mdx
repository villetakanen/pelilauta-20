Semantic colors bridge the gap between the raw **Chroma** tonal scales and functional UI roles. While Chroma represents the "Ink" of the system, Semantic tokens represent the **Intent**.

For a deep-dive into the layering model and how to override Chroma for specific themes, see the [Color System](/principles/color-system/) principle.

## 1. Core Principles: Roles

- **Primary** — Brand identity and primary interactive elements (buttons, links, FABs).
- **Surface** — Architectural backgrounds, cards, sheets, and overlays. 
- **On-[Token]** — Always provides accessible contrast (WCAG AA) for content placed on that background.
