# Elevation Utility

Elevation utilities provide visual depth by combining surface background colors and shadows. Level 1 is strictly a surface color change without a shadow. Levels 2-4 add progressively larger shadows.

## Relative Elevation Engine

When elevated surfaces are nested, the inner surface automatically reduces its shadow to a level relative to its parent. This prevents "harsh" shadow stacking.
