# App Bar Component

The `AppBar` provides context and navigation actions. It supports two main modes: `sticky` (standard page header) and `modal` (dialog header with back navigation).

## Modal Mode

Used for full-screen dialogs or sub-pages.

<AppBar title="Full Title" shortTitle="Short" mode="modal" backHref="#" />

## Sticky Mode

Stays at the top of a scroll container and applies Elevation 2 shadow when content is scrolled beneath it.

<div class="viewport scrollable" style="height: 200px; overflow-y: auto; background: var(--color-surface-4); padding: 1rem; border-radius: var(--cn-border-radius-medium); position: relative;">
  <AppBar title="Sticky AppBar" mode="sticky" />
  <div style="height: 500px; padding: 1rem;">
    <p>Scroll down to see the sticky shadow effect (Elevation 2).</p>
  </div>
</div>
