The `CnIcon` component is the high-fidelity, source-code-first icon primitive for the Cyan Design System. It resolves icons across three tiers of registries and inlines them via SSR.

## Error Handling

When a noun is not found in any registry, the component renders the mandatory **missing glyph**.

    <div style="font-size: 0.75rem;">Unknown Noun</div>
  </div>
</div>
