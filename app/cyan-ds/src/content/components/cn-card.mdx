import CnCard from "@cyan/components/CnCard.svelte";

The `CnCard` component is a flexible container for content snippets, gaming sessions, or character summaries. It supports elevations, responsive covers, and interactive clickability.

## Basic Usage

<div style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: 1fr 1fr; gap: 2rem; background: var(--cn-surface-1); padding: 2rem; border-radius: var(--cn-border-radius-medium);">
  <CnCard elevation={1}>
    <h4 slot="title">Simple Card</h4>
    <p slot="description">This is a basic card with elevation 1.</p>
    Standard card content goes here.
  </CnCard>

  <CnCard elevation={1} href="#">
    <h4 slot="title">Clickable Card</h4>
    <p slot="description">This card transitions on hover.</p>
    Clicking this card navigates to the provided href.
  </CnCard>
</div>
